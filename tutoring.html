Methodology ðŸ”—

I am passionate about helping students succeed in their academic endeavors. One of the tools I use to accomplish this is the Socratic method. By asking students carefully constructed questions, I am able to help students identify their knowledge gaps and discover the correct answer. The goal in using this method is that the student will solve future problems independently using his or her improved critical thinking skills.

I used this method successfully as a teacher assistant for the University of Washington's CSE 331: Software Design and Implementation class. At the end of the academic quarter, students gave me a 4.7 out of 5.0 rating as a teacher assistant.
